About me

I received my B.S from Southeast University from 2011. Then I came to UCLA and recieved my M.S and Ph.D degree in Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Frank Chang at Sept 2016.

I have been working at Qualcomm Inc, mixed signal circuit design department from June 2013 to Sept 2016, since then I joined Kneron Inc, as a hardware architect research scientist. My role at Kneron includes hardware project management, spec neogition with customer, system architecture evaluation and RTL design in the hardware accelerator of Neural Network circuits.

My research interests include mixed signal circuit design and VLSI design for signal processing.
