About me

I received my B.S from Southeast University from 2011. Then I came to UCLA and recieved my M.S and Ph.D degree in Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Frank Chang at Sept 2016.

I have been working at Qualcomm Inc, mixed signal circuit design department from June 2013 to Sept 2016, since then I joined Kneron Inc, as a hardware department primary founder, I'm in charge of spec neogition, system architecture and RTL design in the hardware accelerator of Neural Network circuits.

My research interests include 3D Touch circuit, mixed signal circuit design and VLSI design for signal processing.
